Please use a browser that supports "canvas.

Press the 'p' to switch between Normal Ray Tracing and 4x4 Jittered Anti Alias. Press the 'T' key (SHIFT-t) to ray-trace our first scene. Press the 'E' key to ray-trace our second scene. Use the arrow keys to navigate the WebGL preview. The up arrow key alters height according to tilt. Use the WASD keys to rotate and tilt in the WebGL preview. Press the light buttons to turn them on and off. Put in the x,y,z coordinates to position the lights. The light controls are for the first scene. Reload the trace in order to see the change.
